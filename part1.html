THE MYSTICAL BOOKSHOP of Roslān Tengku

BEING THE FIRST PART OF THE KINGDOM OF THE SEVEN SEAS

The story begins with Baker, a young Paladūrian man whose public mutilation by a Morāthian captor ignites his people’s rebellion against Zal’Morāth. His death sparks a series of supernatural awakenings, connecting the fates of several unlikely allies. Among them is Lilibet, a Morsian girl haunted by dreams of ancient books that reveal an apocalyptic prophecy. Drawn to the mystical bookshop of a reluctant Nusandor noble, Roslān Tengku, she uncovers a hidden truth: a powerful merchant order seeks to erase knowledge that could prevent their prophesied king, the El-Kadāb, from conquering Ëldamoria and her Seven Seas. As dark forces rise, Roslān, Lilibet, and a cast of heroes from across Ëldamoria must each confront their Self and fight to unite—or risk the fall of Ëldamoria to the venegeful Nathduín army and a tyrant’s disasterous reign.
