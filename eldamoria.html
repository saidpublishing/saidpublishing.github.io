THE WORLD OF ËLDAMORIA

Imagine this: a book, buried in the forgotten ruins of Ambar-Paladūr, lost to the world for nearly a thousand years. Then, just a few years ago, it was unearthed—its words like whispers from the past. But here’s where it gets interesting: this isn’t just another ancient story. What they found is something far more extraordinary, and they're calling it: The Chronicles of Eldamoria. A tome, filled to the brim, with tales about our future.
